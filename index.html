How to buy $BitrockInu

1. Create a MetaMask Wallet Bitrock Inu token is available on the Bitrock Chain (BROCK). Metamask is the market leader when it comes to wallets. On Google Chrome, visit metamask.io to download the extension and set up a wallet. On mobile? Get MetaMask's app for iPhone or Android.

2. Send $BROCK to MetaMask Bridge your BROCK from mainnet to Bitrock or transfer it to your MetaMask wallet address from another wallet (e.g. Bitmart).

3. Visit Rock-Swap.io You can currently buy Bitrock Inu on RockSwap.

4. Swap $BROCK for Bitrock Inu Enter the amount of $BROCK you would like to swap for $BitrockInu. Click Connect Wallet then Swap.
